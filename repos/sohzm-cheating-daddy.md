## What it is

Cheating Daddy is an AI-powered personal assistant designed to provide real-time support during video calls and interviews. It captures the user's screen and system audio, feeding it to Google's Gemini 2.0 Flash Live model to generate instant, context-aware suggestions and answers via a transparent, always-on-top overlay.

## Problems solved

- Nervousness or knowledge gaps during high-stakes technical interviews or sales calls
- Need for immediate contextual information without switching windows or losing eye contact
- Difficulty in transcribing and summarizing live meeting segments on the fly

## How to get started

Enter a Gemini API key and select a profile (e.g., Interview, Business Meeting). Click 'Start Session' to launch the transparent overlay. Position the window with keyboard shortcuts; the AI will listen to the interviewer and provide real-time helpful hints on your screen.
