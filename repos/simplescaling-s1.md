## What it is

A research project demonstrating how to achieve state-of-the-art reasoning performance in tiny 1B parameter models through clever data scaling.

## Problems solved

- Belief that reasoning is only for 'big' models; lack of efficient training recipes for edge-device AI; high cost of reasoning research.

## How to get started

Explore the training logs and datasets; run the inference script to test the 's1-1B' model against standard reasoning benchmarks.
