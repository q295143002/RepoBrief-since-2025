# coreyhaines31/marketingskills

### 1. Positioning
Marketing Skills acts as an enhancement plugin for agentic environments specifically tailored for founders and marketers. It injects rigorous growth engineering heuristics into otherwise standard coding/development bots.

### 2. Pain Points & Advantages
Standard LLMs produce lackluster, generic marketing dialogue lacking modern methodologies (like A/B framing or programmatic SEO). This repository fundamentally pivots agent operational directives to mimic highly paid Marketing Directors.

### 3. Technical Architecture
A cross-referencing ecosystem of localized markdown configuration files (`SKILL.md`). Complies strictly with the open `agentskills.io` JSON/Markdown parsing protocol supported by Anthropic and Cursor architectures.

### 4. Core Implementation
Every skill implicitly enforces a prerequisite read of a central `product-marketing-context.md` file. This establishes a master template guaranteeing all subsequent AI copy/analytics operations strictly adhere to customized brand and audience constraints.

### 5. Code Organization
Systematically binned into granular operational domains inside `skills/` tracking categories such as `seo-audit`, `popup-cro`, `pricing-strategy`, and `email-sequence`.

### 6. Quick Start Suggestion
Use `/plugin marketplace add coreyhaines31/marketingskills` within Anthropic's CLI to instantly augment Claude natively, commanding it thereafter to execute `/page-cro` across your application's landing assets.

### 7. Summary
A masterclass toolkit integrating sophisticated corporate engineering and growth-marketing patterns natively into terminal AI interfaces, drastically professionalizing AI-generated copy and strategy execution.
