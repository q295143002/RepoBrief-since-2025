## What it is

NVIDIA's general-purpose foundation model for humanoid robot control and real-world task execution.

## Problems solved

- High difficulty in generalizing robot skills across different hardware
- Lack of a unified 'brain' for humanoid motor control
- Inefficiency in fine-tuning robots for complex physical tasks

## How to get started

Deploy Isaac-GR00T on supported NVIDIA Jetson or workstation hardware to control compatible humanoid robots.
