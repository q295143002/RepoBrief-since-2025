## What it is

Bitchat for Android is a decentralized messaging client that operates entirely over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) mesh networks. It requires no internet, servers, or phone numbers, providing end-to-end encrypted communication in environments where traditional networks are unavailable or untrusted.

## Problems solved

- Reliance on centralized servers and internet connectivity for private messaging
- Vulnerability to cellular network surveillance or shutdowns
- Privacy leaks associated with phone number-based registrations

## How to get started

Download the APK from GitHub or Google Play. Launch the app to automatically start discovering and connecting to nearby bitchat peers (both Android and iOS). Use IRC-style commands like `/j #channel` to join chat rooms or `/m @user` for private messages.
