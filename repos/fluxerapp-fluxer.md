## What it is

Fluxer is a modern, privacy-focused instant messaging and voice-over-IP (VoIP) platform. Designed as an independent alternative to mainstream apps, it focuses on community-building and self-hosting capabilities. The project is currently in early development with a focus on core messaging and group interaction features.

## Problems solved

- Centralized communication risks: Offers a self-hostable, independent alternative to corporate messaging platforms.
- Lack of privacy control: Provides an open-source codebase for community-driven security audits.
- Generic group tools: Tailored specifically for the needs of close-knit friend groups and specialized communities.

## How to get started

The project is currently in the 'coming soon' phase for full documentation and self-hosting guides. Developers can explore the repository to contribute to the platform's core or donate to support its development. Direct usage typically involves connecting to a Fluxer instance once deployed.
