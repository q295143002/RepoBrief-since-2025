## What it is

A state-of-the-art transformer model by Meta AI capable of inferring extremely detailed 3D attributes from multi-view inputs almost instantaneously.

## Problems solved

- Supersedes traditional, highly laborious Structure-from-Motion (SfM) pipelines by accurately resolving intrinsic camera variables alongside depth maps utilizing a unified, lightning-speed neural pass natively.

## How to get started

Utilize pipeline deployment variables natively accessing the transformer explicitly through Hugging Face spaces executing `VGGT.from_pretrained('facebook/VGGT-1B')` rendering directly onto PyTorch operational models seamlessly.
