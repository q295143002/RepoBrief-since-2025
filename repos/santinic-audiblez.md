# santinic/audiblez

### 1. Positioning
santinic/audiblez is positioned as a a cli tool that uses local or remote ai models (like kokoro or openai) to convert documents into high-quality audiobooks with natural prosody. It aims to provide developers with a robust, Python-based solution for generate audiobooks from e-books.

### 2. Pain Points & Advantages
Traditional methods in this domain often suffer from Robotic voices in traditional text-to-speech; high cost of professional audiobook production; difficulty consuming long-form text during chores/commutes.. santinic/audiblez addresses this by a cli tool that uses local or remote ai models (like kokoro or openai) to convert documents into high-quality audiobooks with natural prosody, offering a significant performance boost and better developer ergonomics compared to existing alternatives.
