## What it is

Yupis AI Knowledge Base is a comprehensive, free Chinese resource hub for AI learning, focusing on high-efficiency 'Vibe Coding', tool masterclasses, and AI business strategy.

## Problems solved

- Reduces the 'information gap' for non-technical users in the AI era; provides structured, zero-based learning paths for complex tools like Cursor and Claude Code; offers practical guides for turning AI-built prototypes into profitable products.

## How to get started

Access the knowledge base online at `ai.codefather.cn/vibe`. Browse the 'Vibe Coding' section for absolute beginners or the 'DeepSeek Masterclass' for advanced prompt engineering and local deployment techniques.
