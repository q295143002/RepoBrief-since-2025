## What it is

Dayflow is a macOS application that automatically tracks and categorizes your screen activity to generate a private daily timeline. It uses AI to summarize your work while prioritizing local processing for data privacy.

## Problems solved

- Manual activity logging
- Privacy concerns with cloud-based productivity trackers
- Lack of clear overview of daily work focus

## How to get started

Install the macOS app and grant necessary permissions for screen and activity tracking. Review the automatically generated timeline at the end of the day or export it for further analysis.
