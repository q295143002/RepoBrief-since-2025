## What it is

Awesome Nano Banana Pro is a comprehensive prompt engineering library for the Gemini-powered Nano Banana image model, featuring hyper-realistic, stylistic, and creative prompt patterns.

## Problems solved

- Reduces trial-and-error in high-end AI image generation; solves the 'character consistency' challenge through structured prompt templates; provides specific configurations for era-accurate photography (90s flash
- 2000s mirror selfies) and professional product shots.

## How to get started

Browse the categories in the README (e.g., 'Photorealism', 'Creative Experiments'). Copy the JSON or text-based prompts directly into your Nano Banana or AyeCreate tool, and replace placeholder descriptors with your specific subject requirements.
