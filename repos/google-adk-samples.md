## What it is

The official collection of multi-language sample agents built using Google's Agent Development Kit (ADK) to demonstrate various agentic workflows.

## Problems solved

- Complexity in building production-ready agents; lack of multi-language orchestration examples; difficulty integrating specialized business logic into the ADK framework.

## How to get started

Install ADK; navigate to python/, typescript/, go/, or java/ folders; follow language-specific setup instructions in sub-READMEs.
