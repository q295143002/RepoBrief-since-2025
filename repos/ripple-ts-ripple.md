## What it is

A decentralized communication bus protocol specifically architected for cross-agent coordination.

## Problems solved

- Centralized bottlenecks in agentic communication
- Security risks of third-party command-and-control servers
- Difficulty in managing state across heterogeneous agent fleets

## How to get started

Implement the Ripple protocol in your agents and join the decentralized message bus.
